Scoring Criteria

  • WINNER will get points equal to 10 times the difficulty level s/he played.
  • In case of TIE or DEFEAT, NO POINTS will be awarded to the player.

Note-
  • The AI plays X
  • Score board will be updated after pressing RESET
  • You can't change the difficulty level in the game. If you do so, then it will Restart automatically.
