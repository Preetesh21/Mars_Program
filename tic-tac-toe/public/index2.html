This is the Human vs Human Version where two people play against each other.

Scoring Criteria

  • WINNER will get 10 points.
  • In case of TIE or DEFEAT, NO POINTS will be awarded to the player.

Note-
  • The first player plays X
  • Score board will be updated after pressing RESET
  • Any player can start the game i.e. either 'X' or 'O'.
  • You can't change the difficulty level in the game. If you do so, then it will Restart automatically.
  • To see the updated Highest Scorers, you will have to refresh the page.
